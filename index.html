  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

  3. Add tomato sauce and heavy cream, stir well.
  4. Combine cooked pasta with the sauce.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, then serve hot.

Fluffy Pancakes

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients; stir until just combined.
  4. Heat a skillet over medium heat and grease lightly.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup batter for each pancake; cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook until golden.

Fresh Garden Salad

  • 2 cups mixed greens
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. In a large bowl, combine mixed greens, tomato, cucumber, and red onion.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Drizzle dressing over the salad and toss gently.
  4. Serve immediately.

Simple Omelette

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, ham
  1. In a bowl, beat eggs with milk, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat butter in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  3. Pour in the egg mixture and cook until edges start to set.
  4. Add desired fillings to one half of the omelette.
  5. Fold the omelette over the fillings and cook for another minute.
  6. Slide onto a plate and serve hot.

Berry Banana Smoothie

  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup strawberries
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
  1. Place all ingredients into a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into a glass and enjoy immediately.
